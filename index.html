Content & Customer Engagement

The divisional strategy aims to create an integrated content ecosystem that supports business growth objectives to double the active customer base by FY27 through coordinated efforts across all digital touchpoints.

Strategic Framework

The strategy drives an ambitious 8% revenue CAGR by establishing a balanced approach that supports dual-brand architecture with differentiated positioning across markets. While maintaining consistency, it enables market-specific evergreen execution that addresses unique regional priorities throughout the customer lifecycle - from acquisition to value improvements.

Key Objectives

Double the active customer base by FY27 through an integrated content ecosystem across all digital touchpoints
Drive awareness and adoption of new offerings (crypto, stock trading, OTC options) through coordinated content journeys
Position IG as an innovative leader in financial technology through consistent thought leadership
Establish clear brand differentiation between IG Markets and IG One while maintaining overall brand identity
Deliver sustainable revenue by optimising the complete customer journey from discovery to long-term engagement

Implementation Roadmap

The content development roadmap aligns with the division's product launch timeline in three phases:

Foundation Phase (Q1 FY26)

Establishing fundamental content frameworks while completing Singapore IG One Stock Trading launch

Expansion Phase (Q2-Q3 FY26)

Accelerating content development to support multiple market initiatives including crypto trading rollout

Innovation Phase (Q4 FY26-Q1 FY27)

Extending beyond immediate product launches to support adjacent opportunities and future growth

Strategic Implementation

MARKETING STRATEGY

The dual-brand architecture allocates content focus primarily to IG One's accessible guidance for developing investors (70%), with secondary emphasis on IG Markets' sophisticated tools for experienced traders (20%) and minimal attention to cross-platform thought leadership innovation (10%).
Integrated Channel Approach: IG Markets targets precisely through LinkedIn, newsletters, webinars, and partnerships; IG One reaches broadly via social media, push notifications, educational content, and influencers
Four Content Pillars: Education & Enablement through market-specific content for various experience levels; Product Awareness highlighting competitive advantages; Community Building via success stories and regional influencers; and Market Intelligence delivering actionable investment insights.

KEY ACTIONS

Implement phased content roadmap: Foundation Phase (Q1 FY26) establishes frameworks and launches Singapore IG One Stock Trading; Expansion Phase (Q2-Q3 FY26) accelerates content for crypto trading rollout; Innovation Phase (Q4 FY26-Q1 FY27) extends to adjacent opportunities.
Customer journey optimisation through lifecycle-driven CRM segmentation, strategic SEO keyword architecture, localised ASO metadata and visuals, platform-specific value-focused social media content, and dual-platform multi-format educational ecosystem.
Develop market-specific initiatives: Japan (IG Arena activation, high-value client engagement); Singapore (investing concepts education, first-trade conversion); Australia (stock trading advantages, crypto preparation); UAE (zero-commission trading for diverse audiences).

DEPENDENCIES AND RISKS

Integration of multiple data sources across markets may require substantial technical infrastructure upgrades
Success depends on mature behavioural analytics capabilities for personalisation initiatives
Content quality and consistency requirements across multiple platforms and markets
Technical excellence needed for mobile experience optimisation and structured data implementation
Coordination required between regional teams for market-specific adaptations
Resource allocation across multiple channels may create potential bandwidth limitations
Measurement frameworks needed to track advanced metrics beyond traditional engagement indicators
